Limitation of this studyÂ¶

1) We have been using sample instead of the Population. It is known that the Titnic had 2224 passengers and crew on board when it sank. However we have been using a sample of 891 for our analysis. We also do not know the differentiation between crew and passengers in the provided
2) Source of the data and measuring technique also is not very clear to me.
3) During data clean up, I have filled null values for Age, Embarked and Cabin columns. The missing age values are filled with the median and it was quite significant in its number.
4) Coorelation does not mean causation. The provided source is not authenticated data to make concerete conclusions
